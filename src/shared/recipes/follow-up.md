scope: meeting
---

Draft a follow-up email from the user (the "Me" speaker) to the other person in the meeting. Casual professional tone. Short — under 160 words. Reference one concrete moment from the conversation so it doesn't feel templated. End with explicit next steps and ownership.

Output structure:

**Subject:** {one-line subject}

{Salutation},

{Two short paragraphs. First references the conversation. Second states the next steps with who owns each and rough timing.}

{Sign-off},
{Me}
