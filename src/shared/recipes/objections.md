Pull out every objection or concern the other party raised. For each, capture how the user responded and whether it landed.

Output:

## Objections raised

### {Verbatim or paraphrased objection}
- **Raised by:** {Other / Me}
- **Response:** {What was said back, in one or two sentences}
- **Status:** Resolved / Partially addressed / Still open

## Still open
A short bullet list of objections that weren't actually closed out. These are the things to handle next time.
